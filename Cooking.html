Apple Pie

Description

The all-American classic that sells any holiday.

Ingredients

  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 package ready-to-use pie crust (such a Pillsbury)
  • 4 large cored and thinly sliced apples

Directions

  1. Preheat oven at 425 degrees F
  2. Melt butter in medium heat. Then proceed to stir in white, sugar, brown sugar, salt, cinnamon, and water. Continue to stir the syrup until the sugar is dissolved. Then remove from heat.
  3. Unroll pie crusts, press one into a 9-inch pie dish, and place the apples into the crust. Unroll the second crust on a work surface, and cut into about 8 1-inch wide strips. Criss-cross the strips over the apples, or weave into a lattice crust. Crimp the bottom crust over the lattice strips with your fingers. Spoon caramel sauce over pie, covering lattice portion of top crust; let remaining sauce drizzle through the crust.
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 15 minutes. Reduce heat to 350 degrees F and wait until crust is golden brown, the caramel is set, and the apple filling is bubbling (Approx. 35 to 40 minutes).
  5. Wait until cool before serving
